Navient Complaints Have Taken Significant Drop Since 2020

Written by Bode Ramsay

Untitled chart
Infogram

Navient complaints have taken a significant dip since 2020 compared to the three years prior, according to data collected on the Consumer Finance Database.

As seen in the graph above, the top states in Navient complaints from 2017 to 2020 have all seen their complaints drop significantly below 50% that number from 2020 to now. This trend has been matched by the vast majority of states outside the top 7 as well, which can be seen in the infographics provided by the Consumer Finance Database below.

Infographic on the left (top if you are on mobile) is the number of complaints from each state from 2017-20 , while the infographic on the right displays the same for 2020-23.

Additionally, the two infographics display the regional differences in complaints -- the midwest being the most high-producing area while the northwest seems to be the lowest.
